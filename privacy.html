Golia Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026 · Contact: support@golia.fin

This Privacy Policy explains how Golia protects information when users explore frames, use AI try-on, and participate in the community.

Golia does not create biometric templates or identity databases from uploaded photos.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account information: username, email address, profile photo, bio, age or date of birth, style preferences, saved frames, and other information you choose to provide.
  • Uploaded photos: photos voluntarily submitted for AI eyewear try-on, profile images, community posts, or support requests.
  • AI try-on information: selected frame styles, uploaded references, generation settings, output history, feedback, and non-sensitive interaction signals.
  • Community and purchase data: posts, comments, messages, reports, likes, follows, saves, shares, consumable product identifiers, transaction identifiers, receipt validation data, delivery status, and purchase support records.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use Golia, we may collect device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, IP address, diagnostics, crash logs, network information, and device identifiers where permitted. We may also collect usage data such as pages viewed, eyewear categories browsed, AI try-on activity, saved frames, community interactions, purchase interactions, and search activity.

3. Device Permissions

Golia may request access to camera and photos so you can upload images for eyewear try-on or community sharing. We may request notification permission for account, message, activity, generation, safety, or purchase notices. You can change permissions in device settings at any time.

4. AI Model and User Information Protection

4.1 Internally trained AI model

Golia uses internally managed AI models trained and optimized for eyewear try-on and visual style simulation. The model is designed to place glasses or sunglasses onto user-provided photos, adjust frame scale, align visual position, render lens effects, and blend lighting for a realistic preview.

4.2 No identification use

Golia does not use uploaded photos or AI inputs for face recognition, biometric identification, facial authentication, surveillance, law enforcement profiling, or sensitive attribute inference. We do not create facial biometric templates, faceprints, identity databases, or long-term biometric profiles from uploaded images.

4.3 Limited purpose

Uploaded photos are processed only to provide the requested eyewear try-on, display generated results, support content moderation, troubleshoot service errors, maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and operate the Service.

5. How We Use Information

  • To provide eyewear discovery, AI try-on, frame recommendations, community features, messaging, and account functions.
  • To operate AI features by processing photos, selected frames, generation settings, feedback, and safety signals.
  • To personalize content such as frames, styles, community posts, and recommendations.
  • To process consumable purchases, validate receipts, deliver digital items, prevent fraud, and respond to support requests.
  • To detect harmful content, harassment, impersonation, privacy violations, AI misuse, fraud, spam, and unauthorized access.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers that help operate Golia, including hosting, cloud storage, analytics, crash reporting, customer support, payment verification, content moderation, security, fraud prevention, and infrastructure providers. We may share purchase-related information with app stores and payment platforms. We may disclose information when required by law or when needed to protect users, Golia, or the public. We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense.

7. Consumable Purchase Data

Golia may offer consumable digital items. We may receive purchase-related information such as product identifier, transaction identifier, purchase status, receipt validation data, country, delivery status, and refund or support records. Consumable purchases are not auto-renewable subscriptions and do not automatically renew.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. If you delete your account, we will take steps to delete or anonymize personal information, subject to reasonable exceptions for legal obligations, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, moderation records, security, backups, and compliance.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request portability of certain personal information. You may update profile information in the app, revoke device permissions in device settings, or request account deletion by contacting support@golia.fin.

10. Security and Children’s Privacy

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. The Service is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Contact

If you have questions about this document, AI try-on features, purchases, privacy, reports, or account matters, contact us at support@golia.fin.
