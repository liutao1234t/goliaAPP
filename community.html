Golia Community

Community Guidelines & EULA

Last updated: June 10, 2026 · Contact: support@golia.fin

These guidelines explain how users should behave in Golia’s eyewear inspiration, AI try-on, and community features.

Use Golia respectfully, protect privacy, and only upload content you have permission to use.

1. Community Purpose

Golia is intended to help users explore eyewear styles, preview glasses with AI, share frame inspiration, discuss looks, follow creators, and participate in a positive eyewear-focused community. These guidelines apply to all uploads, AI try-on results, community posts, comments, messages, profile pages, reports, and interactions on Golia.

2. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, threats, hateful conduct, stalking, malicious false reporting, or repeated hostile behavior are prohibited. Users may not attack or degrade people based on protected characteristics.

3. Photo Consent and Privacy

Do not upload another person’s photo, face image, private reference, or profile image without permission. This includes friends, strangers, customers, models, public figures, minors, or people appearing in group photos. You may not share another person’s private information, including addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, private messages, IDs, payment details, or confidential customer information.

4. AI Try-On Rules

Users may not use AI try-on features to create harmful, deceptive, harassing, sexual, exploitative, illegal, impersonating, or privacy-invasive content. Users may not attempt to bypass safety systems, manipulate AI behavior, generate misleading identity-related content, or use AI outputs to defame or deceive others.

5. Eyewear Safety and Product Disclaimer

Golia content is for digital visualization and inspiration. AI try-on results do not confirm product fit, prescription suitability, optical performance, lens safety, or medical appropriateness. Users should consult qualified optical professionals for prescriptions, eye health concerns, and professional fitting advice.

6. Prohibited Content

  • Illegal, harmful, violent, threatening, or exploitative content.
  • Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse.
  • Sexually explicit, pornographic, exploitative, or non-consensual intimate content.
  • Scams, phishing, impersonation, misleading promotions, or counterfeit claims.
  • Content that exposes another person’s private information.
  • Copyright-infringing photos, logos, product images, or artwork.
  • Spam, bots, fake engagement, scraping, or platform manipulation.

7. Intellectual Property

Users should only upload photos, eyewear images, logos, product references, or creative materials that they own or are authorized to use. Unauthorized use of copyrighted photos, brand logos, product images, celebrity images, or third-party materials may result in removal or account enforcement.

8. Messaging and Style Interaction

Messaging and community interactions must remain respectful and style-focused. Users may not harass creators, pressure users, solicit illegal services, send spam, request sensitive personal data, or use chat features to abuse others.

9. Consumable Purchases and Fair Use

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with platform rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, manipulate item delivery, abuse refunds, or use purchased items to harass or manipulate others.

10. Reporting and Moderation

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, AI misuse, privacy violations, copyright infringement, impersonation, harassment, scams, misleading product claims, or other misuse. Golia may review reports using manual review, automated tools, safety signals, purchase records, moderation history, and community reports.

11. Enforcement

Golia may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, messaging restrictions, AI generation restrictions, purchase restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes.

Contact

If you have questions about this document, AI try-on features, purchases, privacy, reports, or account matters, contact us at support@golia.fin.
